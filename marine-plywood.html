Marine Plywood : IS-710

A Global Standard In Marine Plywood Industry

Multi Strong Plywood is a leading (IS: 710 certified) Marine Plywood Manufacturer. The offered marine plywood is designed primarily to resist deteriorating in a high-moisture climate conditions. Therefore, the finished plywood is chemical tested through water-soluble preservatives beneath vacuum arm-twisting to ensure safety against wood eliminating organisms and to meet the product insects, borer, termites and fungi-free.

Special Features Of Our Marine Plywood:

1) It has extreme tenacity due to retaining a thicker facade.
2) It assures no water will be tangled in the sheet voids.
3) It is capable of resisting alternate wetting and drying situations.
4) Bonded with Phenol-Formaldehyde (PF) resin
5) It is capable of enduring drastic climate situations.
6) It can withstand distortion, delaminating or flexing, which usually compels too considerably moistness that damages the timbers.
7) It has a flawless finish and has exceptional bonding to plies.

The provided marine plywood can customize sizes as per the requirement of our customers. Our valuable customers can also obtain various types of Plywood from us at leading market prices.
